## Alan Alda's The 3 Rules of 3
https://www.bookofthrees.com/alan-alda-the-3-rules-of-3/

Tell them what you're going to tell them. Tell them. Tell them what you told them.

## Text Size
Get up from your desk and stand ten feet or more away from your monitor. The smallest text should still be readable by a person at the back of the room.

## Acronyms and Abbreviations
Avoid them. If you must use them, define them the first time you use them. If you have to use an acronym in a slide, nonetheless try to speak the full words, from time to time.

## Calm them down
Show or tell something fun right at the beginning. Let people know that they've come to the right place.

## Call to action
The last slide will probably remain visible during the Q&A. It should contain your call to action, your name, your email address, and your website.

## Practice
Practice, practice, practice.
