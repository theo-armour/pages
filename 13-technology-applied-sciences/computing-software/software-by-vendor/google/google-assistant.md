## Tips and Tricks

* "Call Grandma **on speakerphone**"
* "Set volume to 3"
* "What is the S&P 500|| BTC trading at?"
* "Play NPR news summary"
* "Tell me a joke || make me laugh || fun fact"
* "Read me a poem"

## Google Assistant Routines (Built-in Feature)

Open the Google App or Google Home App.

Tap your Profile Picture in the top right.

Tap Settings > Google Assistant.

Scroll down and tap Routines.

Tap the "+" (New) button to create a new shortcut/routine
