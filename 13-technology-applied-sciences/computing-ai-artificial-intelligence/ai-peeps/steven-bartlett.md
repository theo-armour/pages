# Steven Bartlett


Steven Bartlett is a British entrepreneur, author, and public speaker. He is best known for being the founder and former CEO of Social Chain, a global social media marketing agency. Bartlett has been recognized for his innovative approach to business and his ability to build successful companies from the ground up. He has also written a book titled "Happy Sexy Millionaire," which shares his insights on entrepreneurship and personal development. Bartlett is a prominent figure in the business world and has been featured in various media outlets for his achievements and contributions to the industry.
