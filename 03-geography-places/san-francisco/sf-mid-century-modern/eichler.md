## Key Characteristics of Eichler Homes:

* https://share.google/aimode/aqWZRNUMUt7FZUpNy

Architectural Style: California Modern, inspired by Frank Lloyd Wright.
Construction: Post-and-beam framing allowing open spaces and varied rooflines (often low-pitched or A-framed).
Indoor-Outdoor Living: Floor-to-ceiling glass walls, atriums, courtyards, and private patios.
Interior Design: Open floor plans, integrated furniture, radiant floor heating, and natural materials like wood paneling.
Exterior: Clean geometric lines, few street-facing windows, and a focus on blending with nature.
Social Impact: Affordable housing for diverse communities, a rarity for the era.
