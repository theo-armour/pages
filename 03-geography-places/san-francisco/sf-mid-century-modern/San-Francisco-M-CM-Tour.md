## Stops
1. **The Masonic** (1111 California St) - Geometric marble masterpiece (1958).
2. **St. Mary's Cathedral** (1111 Gough St) - Hyperbolic paraboloid roof (1971).
3. **Mel's Drive-In** (3355 Geary Blvd) - MCM "Googie" Dining.
4. **Maiden Lane (FLW)** (155 Maiden Ln) - Frank Lloyd Wright spiral ramp (1948).
5. **One Bush Plaza** (1 Bush St) - SF's first glass curtain-wall tower (1959).
6. **Heath Ceramics** (2900 18th St) - Iconic MCM design & tile.
7. **Foreign Cinema** (2534 Mission St) - Modernist dinner & film.
