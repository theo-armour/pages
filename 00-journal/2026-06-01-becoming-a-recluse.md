I'm very happy living here at Heritage on the Marina. Much as I loved the idea of Paris and Venice and perhaps a few other places. I don't really have any desire to actually go there.

What I'm really enjoying is a certain amount of perhaps boredom.I have the same schedule. I eat the same food. I dress the same way. I work every day.When I'm doing this, my weight is good, and I exercise and I take care of myself. When there is a break, I put on weight. I drink too much and can become a little discombobulated.

On the other hand, I do love visits. A few hours of deep talk with somebody is always strengthening.

At the same time, I'm still as driven as I ever have been and feel the need to complete as many things as possible before the brain starts to wither even more.
