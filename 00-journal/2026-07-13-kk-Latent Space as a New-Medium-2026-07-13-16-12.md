# 2026-07-13 KK Latent Space as a New Medium

https://substack.com/inbox/post/206366433

In this post, Kelly explores the high-order compression of human knowledge called latent space. He looks at large language models as being possible media types.
He identifies multiple patterns. prototyping, White space discovery, cross-domain analogy, latent space measurement, mining meta-patterns, trajectories, retro-latents, latent space infiltrators, anomaly detection, simulated reality, parallel worlds, latent epistemology.

At the end of the article, Kelly throws down the gauntlet. He says, "And the most likely, the greatest of them of all, will be the ones I've not thought of here."

Kelly, when you go out on a limb like this, how can you forget not going out with the heroes: Nassim Taleb's "Latent black Swans", or RichardsoDonald Rumsfeld's or "Latent unknown unknowns"??
