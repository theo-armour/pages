# 2026-07-13 KK Latent Space as a New Medium

https://substack.com/inbox/post/206366433

In this post, Kelly explores the high-order compression of human knowledge calling it latent space. He considers large language models as possible new media types. He names multiple patterns: prototyping, white-space discovery, cross-domain analogy, latent-space measurement, mining meta-patterns, trajectories, retro-latents, latent-space infiltrators, anomaly detection, simulated reality, parallel worlds, and latent epistemology.

At the end of the article, Kelly throws down the gauntlet. He says, "And the most likely, the greatest of them of all, will be the ones I've not thought of here."

Kelly, that is quite a gauntlet: the greatest possibilities are the ones you have not yet thought of. But surely you haven’t forgotten the usual heroes waiting in the wings—Taleb’s black swans or Rumsfeld’s unknown unknowns, both latent and ready to surprise us flying out of left field.
